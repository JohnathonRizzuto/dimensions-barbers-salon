Our Story

A Salon & Barber
For Everyone.

Dimensions Barbers & Salon has been the neighborhood hair stop in Minooka for years. We cut, color, style, and trim for everyone in the family — dads and kids in for a fade, moms in for a foil, grandparents in for their regular trim.

It's a single chair philosophy: take the time, talk to the client, deliver a result you can stand behind. That's what's kept Minooka coming back.

"One shop. Every family. Every chair."
